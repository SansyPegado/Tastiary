Japanese Recipes


Dish Description Recipe
Sushi Sushi is vinegared rice served with raw or cooked seafood and vegetables.
  1. Cook sushi rice and season with rice vinegar.
  2. Place on nori (seaweed) sheet, add fish or veggies.
  3. Roll tightly and cut into slices.
Ramen Ramen is a Japanese noodle soup with broth, meat, and toppings.
  1. Boil noodles separately.
  2. In a pot, simmer chicken broth, soy sauce, garlic, and ginger.
  3. Add noodles, top with boiled egg, spring onions, and sliced meat.
Yakitori Yakitori are skewered grilled chicken pieces.
  1. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Thread onto skewers and grill.
  3. Brush with sauce made from soy sauce, mirin, sugar, and sake.
