SeaFood Description Recipe
Grilled Salmon Rich in omega-3, salmon is flavorful and perfect for grilling or baking.
  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season salmon with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  3. Grill for 6-8 minutes per side until cooked through.
  4. Serve with a side of steamed vegetables.
Fish Fry (Pomfret) A crispy coastal Indian favorite, especially in Goa and Kerala.
  1. Marinate pomfret with turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
  2. Coat in rice flour for extra crispiness.
  3. Fry in hot oil until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Serve with lemon wedges and onion rings.
Spicy Crab Curry Crabs cooked in coconut-based spicy masala, popular in coastal India.
  1. Clean and cut crabs into pieces.
  2. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in coconut oil.
  3. Add spices (turmeric, chili powder, coriander) and tomatoes.
  4. Add crabs and cook until they turn red and are fully cooked.
  5. Serve with steamed rice or appams.
