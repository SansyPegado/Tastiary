Italian Recipes


Dish Description Recipe
Margherita Pizza Classic Neapolitan pizza with tomatoes, mozzarella, and fresh basil.
  1. Spread tomato sauce over rolled-out dough.
  2. Add mozzarella slices and basil leaves.
  3. Drizzle olive oil and bake at 220°C (428°F) for 10–15 minutes.
Spaghetti Pasta tossed with eggs, Pecorino cheese, pancetta, and black pepper.
  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente.
  2. In a pan, sauté sliced garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add chili flakes, then toss in the pasta.
  4. Mix well, garnish with parsley, and serve.
Tiramisu A classic coffee-flavored Italian dessert.
  1. Dip ladyfingers in coffee and layer in dish.
  2. Mix mascarpone with egg yolks and sugar; layer over fingers.
  3. Repeat layers and chill. Dust with cocoa before serving.
