Butter Chicken

Origin: India

Prep Time: 20 mins   |   Cook Time: 40 mins

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (boneless)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 onions (chopped)
  • 3 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt & chili powder

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices for 1 hour.
  2. Sauté onions, then add tomato puree.
  3. Add chicken and cook until done.
  4. Stir in cream and butter, simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
