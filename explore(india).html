Indian Recipes


Dish Description Recipe
Masala Dosa South Indian crispy crepe with spicy potato filling.
  1. Ferment rice-dal batter.
  2. Cook dosa on tawa.
  3. Fill with spiced mashed potatoes.
Chole Spicy chickpea curry from North India.
  1. Pressure cook chickpeas.
  2. Cook masala base with onions, tomatoes, and spices.
  3. Simmer cooked chickpeas in the masala.
Butter Chicken Grilled chicken in a buttery tomato cream sauce.
  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices.
  2. Grill or pan-fry until cooked.
  3. Make creamy tomato sauce with butter and cream.
  4. Combine grilled chicken with sauce and simmer.
