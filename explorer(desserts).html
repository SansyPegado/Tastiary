Delicious Dessert


Dessert Description Recipe
Chocolate Mug Cake Quick single-serving chocolate cake made in a microwave mug.
  1. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, and salt in a mug.
  2. Add milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla; stir until smooth.
  3. Microwave for 1 minute. Let cool slightly before eating.
Gulab Jamun Soft milk-solid balls soaked in rose-flavored sugar syrup, a classic Indian dessert.
  1. Make dough with khoya, flour, and baking soda; form small balls.
  2. Fry until golden brown. Soak in warm sugar syrup flavored with cardamom and rose water.
  3. Let soak for at least 30 minutes before serving.
Mango Sticky Rice Thai dessert made with sticky rice, coconut milk, and fresh mango slices.
  1. Cook sticky rice and mix with sweetened coconut milk.
  2. Serve with ripe mango slices on the side.
  3. Drizzle with extra coconut milk if desired.
