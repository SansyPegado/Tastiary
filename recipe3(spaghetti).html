Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Origin: Italy

Prep Time: 10 mins   |   Cook Time: 15 mins

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Grated parmesan (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Drain, reserving 1/4 cup of pasta water.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic until golden.
  3. Add chili flakes, cooked pasta, and reserved water. Toss well.
  4. Season with salt, garnish with parsley and parmesan.
  5. Serve hot with crusty bread or salad.
