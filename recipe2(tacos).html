Classic Mexican Tacos

Origin: Mexico

Prep Time: 15 mins   |   Cook Time: 15 mins

Ingredients:

  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Cheddar cheese (grated)
  • Sour cream or salsa

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in a pan until fragrant.
  2. Add ground beef, cook until browned. Add cumin, paprika, salt & pepper.
  3. Warm tortillas on a skillet or microwave.
  4. Fill tortillas with meat, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and toppings of choice.
  5. Serve immediately with lime wedges and enjoy!
